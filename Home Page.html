Combatting Misinformation for Women and Gender Minorities


What is Fake News?
False stories that appear to be news, spread on the internet or using other media, usually created to influence political views or as a joke.


Misinformation on Social Media Article Links

Here are a few fact checking websites you can use:
  • Snopes: One of the oldest and most trusted fact-checking websites.
    Snopes
  • Reuters Fact Check: This fact-checking branch of Reuters news agency verifies viral social media content, images, and news claims to stop the spread of false information globally.
    Reuters Fact Check
  • Agence France-Presse (AFP): Provides fact checks on viral news stories and social media claims from around the world, with a focus on international news and misinformation.
    AFP Fact Check
  • Lead Stories: Verifies viral news and social media posts with a focus on debunking hoaxes and misleading stories.
    Lead stories
